Welcome to the Nest

The Pigeon Cafe® is the ultimate spot for bird lovers. Yes, we're calling all pigeon fans like us!

Visit the only pigeon-themed café in Manchester – maybe even the world! Over 100 feathery friends, great food, and warm vibes await you.

And don’t worry, your coffee is safe… probably. But hey, what’s happiness without a little bird on your shoulder?

The Pigeon Cafe® is a re-homing and care facility for pigeons. From a range of different backgrounds. From rescue pigeons, to strays, to pigeons which have had medical issues.
— We take them in and look after them —

Our food menu

All of our food is prepared by our team of trained kitchen staff from the open kitchen where you can see your food being made.

We let our pigeons get involved — they are our head of tasting.
